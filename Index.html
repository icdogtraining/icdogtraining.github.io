One-on-one training sessions
I come to you.

We start with an initial consultation of 90 minutes to identify your needs, your dog’s needs and address problem behaviours.

I will provide a management and training plan.

I will teach you how to train your dog in behaviours such as stay, recall, leave, loose lead walk, etc. with fun and easy methods.

We will address problem behaviours like chewing, jumping up, mouthing, barking, digging, etc.

You will learn you about toilet training, home alone, dog body language, polite manners, enrichment, multi-pet households, equipment, or any other topic you would like to learn about.

Subsequent sessions are an hour long and include follow-ups on training behaviours and addressing problems.

Isabel is a Delta dog trainer and an accredited member of the Association of Pet Dog Trainers (APDT) and the Delta Institute. She only uses positive reinforcement techniques and methods backed by science and research. She regularly attends professional development seminars, workshops and conferences to keep her knowledge up to date.

Isabel grew up in her parents’ farm in Argentina, where she trained horses for show-jumping and competed in this sport as a hobby for many years. She also has a Master’s Degree in Translation and worked in the U.K for over a decade. She discovered the world of dog training and agility after attending Discover Dogs in London and became very keen to learn how to train dogs ever since.

After moving to Australia with her family, she completed her Certificate IV in Companion Animal Services with the Delta Institute and now works as a full-time dog trainer in Sydney’s Eastern Suburbs.

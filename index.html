About
Isabel is a Delta accredited dog trainer and member of the Pet Professional Guild Australia (PPGA), the Delta Institute and the Association of Pet Dog Trainers (APDT). Additionally, she is a licenced Family Paws® Parent Educator, a Family Dog Mediator® and a MindDog Trainer (psychiatric assistance dogs). She is a force-free trainer who only uses methods and techniques backed by science and research, regularly attends professional development seminars, workshops and conferences, and volunteers as a trainer at Sydney All Breeds Dog Training Club (SABDTC) in Concord.

Isabel grew up in her parents’ farm in Argentina, where she trained horses for show-jumping and competed in this sport for many years. She has a Master’s Degree in Translation and worked in the U.K for over a decade.

After moving to Australia with her family, she completed her Certificate IV in Companion Animal Services with the Delta Institute and attained her certification as a Family Dog Mediator® and Family Paws® Parent Educator. She now works full-time as a dog trainer in Sydney’s eastern suburbs.
